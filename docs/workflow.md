# Workflow and Status Tracker

## Service Lifecycle
1. **Request Submitted**
   - Customer submits service form.
   - Service number is generated and sent via email.

2. **Shipment In Transit**
   - Customer ships items with service number referenced.
   - Optional: tracking number captured in portal.

3. **Checked in**
   - Service team confirms receipt.
   - Intake checklist completed, photos uploaded.

4. **Cleaning In Progress**
   - Technicians update progress notes.

5. **Quality Review**
   - QA checklist and inspection report generated.

6. **Ready for Return**
   - Invoice generated and published.
   - Shipping label created.

7. **Completed**
   - Items shipped back and service closed.

## Customer Portal Views
- **Tracker**: Domino's-style horizontal or vertical milestone tracker.
- **Details**: Service summary, item list, shipping info.
- **Documents**: Only published documents visible.

## Admin Actions
- Update status and set public/private notes.
- Upload documents and set publish state.
- Trigger customer notifications manually or automatically.
